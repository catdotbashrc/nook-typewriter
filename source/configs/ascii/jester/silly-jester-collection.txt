## Design Philosophy:
- Big expressive eyes for emotions
- Exaggerated expressions
- Silly poses and gestures
- Medieval bells represented by ~.~
- Diamond patterns (â™¦) for traditional jester outfit
- Simple shapes that render well on E-Ink
- Each has a personality and purpose
- Intentionally derpy and endearing

## E-Ink Optimization Notes:
- Uses basic ASCII characters
- Avoids fine details
- High contrast design
- No gradient characters
- Works at small sizes
- Animations use simple frame swaps
