## Design Notes:
- Uses simple ASCII characters that render well on E-Ink
- Avoids fine details that might blur on 6-inch display
- Bell-cap design with ·:· pattern for jester hat
- Diamond (♦) represents jester's traditional diamond pattern
- 'd' and 'b' create simple hands
- Expressions change based on context
- Chibi proportions (large head, small body)
